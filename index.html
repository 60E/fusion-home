FusionCoin

Hybrid PoW with both SHA256 and Scrypt. Merged Mining with BitCoin, LiteCoin and other altercoins.

Introducing FusionCoin

FusionCoin is a hybrid Proof-of-Work(PoW) crypto currency which support both SHA256(BitCoin) and Scrypt(LiteCoin) hash algorithm. FusionCoin block chain thus contains two kinds of block with individual target to meet with. Like Namecoin and other altercoins, FusionCoin can be merged mined with either BitCoin or Litecoin based altercoins.

Hybrid PoW

Fusion accept PoW both in SHA256 and Scrypt. Your mining rig is ready to go.

Merged Mining

You can mine FusionCoin while mining BitCoin, LiteCoin or other altercoins.

Active Development

The FusionCoin is on its infancy, we are actively implmenting new features including improved script system, messaging and more.
