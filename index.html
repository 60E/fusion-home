FusionCoin

Hybrid PoW with both SHA256 and Scrypt. Merged Minable with BitCoin, LiteCoin and other altercoins.

Introducing FusionCoin

FusionCoin is a P2P Crypto Currency with a hybrid proof of work(PoW) system. FusionCoin supports two hashing algorithm: SHA256 and Scrypt, respectively used for the well-known BitCoin and LiteCoin. Each algorithm has its seperate difficulty, both targetting 4 minutes. Like Namecoin and other altercoins, FusionCoin can be merged mined with either BitCoin or Litecoin based altercoins.

Hybrid PoW

FusionCoin accepts PoW both in SHA256 and Scrypt. Your mining rig is ready to go.

Merged Mining

You can mine FusionCoin while mining BitCoin, LiteCoin or other altercoins.

Active Development

FusionCoin is on its infancy, we are actively improving it while implmenting new features including enhanced script system, message and more.
